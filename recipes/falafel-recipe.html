Home Made falafel Recipes

A traditional Middle Eastern dish, falafel are made from ground chickpeas and deep-fried to give them a satisfying, crunchy coating. Learn how to make falafel at home with this easy step-by-step guide and get prepped for shopping with the ingredients list at the bottom.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this top-rated homemade falafel recipe:

  • 2 x 400g tins chickpeas
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Pinch of allspice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 4 tbsp plain flour, plus 50g extra for coating
  • Large bunch fresh parsley
  • Large bunch fresh coriander
  • Sunflower oil, for frying

steps:

  1. Make the falafel mixture: Blend half the chickpeas with 2 tbsp brine, spring onions, garlic, spices, salt, baking powder, flour, parsley, and coriander. Add the remaining chickpeas and pulse briefly to keep texture. Adjust consistency if needed.
  2. Chill: Transfer mixture to a bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Make the houmous: Blend chickpeas, tahini, water, garlic, salt, olive oil, lemon juice, and paprika. Add herbs and blend again. Chill until needed.
  4. Shape the falafels: Form into 14–16 balls, flatten slightly, and coat with flour.
  5. Fry the falafel: Heat oil in a pan and fry patties in batches for 3–4 minutes per side until golden. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve: Let falafel cool slightly, then serve with herby houmous.
