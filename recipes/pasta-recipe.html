Home Made Pasta Recipes

Fancy making your own pasta? No machine required. You can use plain flour to make the pasta, but 00 flour gives it a little more bite. This pasta is called pappardelle, the thick, silky ribbons pair really well with everything - from hearty sauces to a simple pesto.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this top-rated homemade Pasta recipe:

  • 350g 00 flour
  • 3 medium eggs, plus 3 egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Semolina, for dusting
  • 1 tbsp salt

steps:

  1. Mix flour and eggs: Make a well in the flour, add eggs, yolks, and oil. Gradually mix to form a rough dough.
  2. Knead the dough: Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Divide and rest: Shape into a cylinder, wrap in clingfilm, and rest for 1â€“3 hours.
  4. Roll out pasta: Flatten one piece at a time into 1mm thick sheets. Dust with semolina between layers.
  5. Slice sheets: Fold sheets, cut into strips, and dust with semolina to prevent sticking.
  6. Cook the pasta: Boil salted water, cook pasta for 4 minutes in batches, then drain and serve.
